2. Data Collected and Purpose of Processing

GPTalk is an AI-based platform that provides all members of the University of Hohenheim with easy access to generative artificial intelligence (AI). The aim is to promote the integration of AI into work, teaching, and research processes and to create a space for exchange, reflection, and collaboration. Login is via the university user ID.

The platform enables access to various AI models, including OpenAI (ChatGPT) and open-source models provided by the Gesellschaft für wissenschaftliche Datenverarbeitung mbH Göttingen (GWDG).

2.1 Access Data / Server Log Files

When accessing GPTalk, the University of Hohenheim's web server processes the following data:

  • Browser type and language
  • Operating system used
  • Referrer URL (previously visited page)
  • Date and time of the server request
  • Transferred data volume and access status

This data is used for system security and website provision and is automatically deleted after 8 days.

2.2 Authentication and Access Control

Login is via the central Hohenheim user ID. The access credentials are used solely for authentication and are not stored.

2.3 Storage of Chat Histories

GPTalk 2 enables the storage of individual and group chats to support continuous usage and collaborative workflows. The data is stored exclusively on the servers of the University of Hohenheim.

  • Purpose of storage: Improving usability, resuming conversations, collaboration in group rooms, and documentation of work processes.
  • Stored personal data: User ID, name, email address, group affiliation (e.g., student, staff), message timestamps, and metadata (e.g., selected model, system prompts). The contents of chats (prompts & responses) are encrypted and unreadable by the server.
  • Access rights: Only authorized users have access to their own individual chats and group rooms they belong to. Authorization is granted via a private key stored only locally on the user's device.
  • Deletion: Users can delete their individual and group chats independently.

3. Use of External AI Services via GPTalk

GPTalk provides access to AI language models from external providers. Different conditions apply depending on the provider:

3.1 No Transfer of Personal Data

  • No IP addresses or user IDs are transmitted to external providers.

3.2 Use of GWDG Models (Open Source)

  • Processing takes place on servers of the GWDG.
  • Contents (prompts and responses) are not stored.
  • Only the time and length of the request/output are recorded for billing purposes—without any content-related information.

3.3 Use of ChatGPT Models (OpenAI)

  • Content of requests (prompts and responses) is stored for a maximum of 30 days, exclusively to detect and prevent misuse.
  • After that, the data is automatically deleted.
  • The content is not used for training purposes.

4. Use of Cookies

GPTalk uses technically necessary session cookies. These are automatically deleted when the browser is closed.

5. Your Rights as a User

According to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Access (Art. 15 GDPR) – Insight into your stored data
  • Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) – Correction of incorrect data
  • Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) – Deletion of personal data under certain conditions
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Objection (Art. 21 GDPR)

To exercise your rights, please contact the Data Protection Officer of the University of Hohenheim.
